Pristinum Meet is a professional video conferencing platform that offers a range of features to enhance your online meetings. Our HD post-call recordings ensure that you never miss a detail, allowing you to focus on the conversation without worrying about taking notes.

Features

  • Crystal-Clear Recordings— All meetings are captured in high definition, providing clean, polished recordings that are ready to share or review. Each meeting is preserved in sharp detail, independent of live stream hiccups or network glitch. Your internet may struggle—but your recording won’t.
  • Rewind While Live— Missed something? Instantly rewind any point during the live meeting without disrupting the flow.
  • Designed for Professionals— With a clean interface and powerful features, Pristinum brings reliability, clarity, and confidence to every conversation.
